Who are we?

T-Space is a team of 3 young and courageous students who are passionate about mental health issues. We aim to encourage and promote mental health and psychological well-being while protecting the privacy of our users. We provide various methods to talk about your experiences which include a censored and kind section where you can write it down and a professional platform where you can talk with experts via audio or video chat. We welcome all students with open arms to use our platform, get help wherever needed and emerge healthy. So take a deep breath, pick a cool nickname and get started...

P.S. We value your privacy and your wish to keep your identity hidden. You will only be known by your nickname and no personal data will be shared with anyone else, not even your therapist.
