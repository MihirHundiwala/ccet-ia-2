About Us

Mental health is a rising issue in our society. In India, only 7.3% of its 365 million youth report such problems. And yet, we keep pretending this is not happening in our own homes, schools and colleges. Public stigma associated with mental health problems particularly affects help-seeking among young people. Share your Stories!

We all have a story to tell, even if it is tainted by scars. They are nothing to hide away from, these journeys should be shared and celebrated instead.

We have a place where you can share your struggles, problems, thoughts, journeys, learnings and much more: "Stories". As the name suggests, it is a platform for you to share your story and help others gain motivation and inspiration from you. Sharing your coping methods and solutions can help a lot many people out there who might be suffering from the same symptoms as you were.

"Stories" is not just a platform for survivors, it is a platform to vent out your feelings and emotions: sad or happy.

Facts

Many people feel worried and dejected every now and then, but when does it take over their whole lives? Losing a loved one, doing poorly in school or work, being bullied and other hardships might lead a person to feel lonely, scared, or anxious. Some people experience this on an everyday basis, sometimes even for no reason at all.
